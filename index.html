📌 Factor Guide

🚦 Tier Boundary Reminder
  • Tier 1: no meaningful issue for that factor.
  • Tier 2: issue exists, but all key information remains readable/extractable.
  • Tier 3: issue prevents reliable extraction.
🏥 Medical Device Special Rules
  • Focus on the displayed measurement.
  • Blood pressure: systolic + diastolic.
  • Glucose meter: glucose value.
  • Weight scale: body mass.
  • Orientation includes display rotation, not just perspective.
  • Text Structure Tier 2 means values visible but units missing.

🖼️ Example-Based Practice

Scenario cards modeled from the guideline examples and edge cases: clear vs low contrast, minor glare vs unreadable glare, one device vs multiple unclear devices, units missing vs key readings missing.
